About

Esme Magee-Wood is a British figurative painter both living and working in London. Her recurring subjects regularly involve capturing the everyday elements of relaxing figures. Through her impressive use of colour and mark-making, she is able to create captivating compositions of serene moments.

Originally raised in Surrey, Esme went on to study for a BA in Illustration and Visual Media at London College of Communication. Since graduating in 2020, her commercial work as a freelance illustrator and graphic designer has been varied. Notably, she has collaborated with musicians such as pop artist Anne-Marie, acoustic musician Taves, and hip-hop artist Tommy Saint, designing logos and painted album covers in her distinctive style.

Currently, Esme works as a Painting Assistant in an artist studio for Priest, an East London-based contemporary artist. Esme primarily works painting with oil on canvas, but also includes print media, using both lino and mono prints.

This website was set up to showcase her artwork.
